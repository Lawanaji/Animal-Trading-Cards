Arctic Fox

Arctic fox fur changes seasonally. Fluffy white coats in the winter; brownish or grayish in the summer:

  • Scientific Name: Vulpes Lagopus
  • Average Lifespan: 7 times 10
  • lenght: Up to 110 centimeters
  • Habitat: Arctic and alpine tundra

A small fox well adapted to living in cold environments, and is best known for its thick, warm fur that is also used as camouflage.
